Game Controls

  • 🕹️ Move the platform by scrolling it left or right
  • 🎯 Single tap will shoot the ball with lowest charge
  • 🟦 Blue indicator below platform lit up when charged
  • ☄️ Move ball by physical interaction with platform
  • 🔌 Tap and hold to increase platform charge
  • 💫 Release to launch the ball
  • 💥 Break crates to score points
  • ⚡️ Chain hits for multipliers
  • 🔓 Complete levels to unlock more

Tips & Tricks

  • Time your shots carefully for maximum impact
  • Use the charge mechanic for powerful shots
  • Chain multiple hits to increase your score multiplier
  • Watch out for special power-ups and bonuses
