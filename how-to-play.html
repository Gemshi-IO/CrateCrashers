Tips & Tricks

  • To move the ball left or right, time the bounce of the ball and move the platform at that moment. The physics will change the direction of the ball, similar to how moving a tennis racket affects the ball's trajectory.
  • Time your shots carefully for maximum impact
  • Use the charge mechanic for powerful shots
  • Chain multiple hits to increase your score multiplier
